14. 1Sebutkan masing-masing fungsi dari may,must and should


Jawaban:

May = lesser possibility --> tidak terlalu harus / kebolehan

Must = definite --> harus

Should = definite --> seharusnya

Penggunaan:

Must --> used when expressing obligation or an unavoidable requirement. "Must" digunakan untuk menjalankan aturan atau tidak bisa dihindari (harus banget)

Should --> more of a recommendation. "Should" digunakan lebih untuk rekomendasi atau untuk mencapai tujuan (harus, tapi lebih pada saran/rekomendasi)

1. May (mungkin, boleh)

dipakai untuk menyatakan :

• Permohonan izin

ex : May I borrow your motorcycle?

• Permohohonan atau harapan

ex : May you both full of happiness

2. Must (harus, mesti)

dipakai untuk menyatakan :

• Keharusan/mesti

ex : You must go now

3. Should

dipakai untuk menyatakan :

• Keharusan

ex : He should study hard

• Anjuran (artian sebaiknya)

ex : You are ill, you should go to the doctor

•Bentuk lampau dari shall

ex : When he came to my house I should go

19. dialog modal verbs should,must,have to,can,may


Writing Dialog Based on Modal Verbs (Should, Must, Have to, Can and May)Jawaban
Pendahuluan

Sebelum kita membuat dialognya, terlebih dahulu kalian harus memahami cara penggunaan dari modal verbs tersebut. Berikut ini akan kakak uraikan satu-persatu.


Should

1. Digunakan untuk memberikan saran, rekomendasi atau anjuran.

Contoh: You hair is too long. You should get a haircut.


2. Mengungkapkan situasi yang harusnya terjadi di waktu tertentu

Contoh: He should have the letter by now. I sent it a couple of weeks ago.


3. Memprediksi sesuatu akan terjadi di masa depan

Contoh: It should be fine tomorrow. I checked the forecast.


4. Mengungkapkan kewajiban namun terdengar lebih sopan

Contoh: You should pay more attention in class


5. Diharapkan namun tidak terjadi (should + have + past participle)

Contoh: I should have studied more but I was too tired


6. Tidak melakukan kewajiban (should + be + verb-ing)

Contoh: We should be studying for the test


7. Untuk menganjurkan agar tidak melakukan sesuatu karena itu tidak baik atau salah (Shouldnn’t)

Contoh: You shouldn’t talk like that you grandmother.


Must

1. Untuk mengungkapkan kewajiban bisa juga diaplikasikan pada peraturan dalam hukum

Contoh: I must stay inside the house when there is thunderstorm


2. Untuk mempertegas kebutuhan akan sesuatu

Contoh: Humans must have drinking water at least 8 glass per day


3. Meyankinkan bahwa sesuatu benar adanya

Contoh: Look at all of that snow. It must be really cold outside.


4. Mengungkapkan asumsi logis yang positif (must + have + past participle)

Contoh: She must have been at her home; her car was there.


5. Anjuran yang lebih kuat daripada menggunakan should

Contoh: We really must get together for dinner sometime.


Have to

Digunakan untuk mengganti kata must agar terdengar lebih familiar. Have to digunakan untuk mengungkapkan kewajiban eksternal.


- You have to study

- They have to wait for the result

- We have to complete the essay by Friday


Can

1. Untuk mengungkapkan kemampuan (secara fisik) atau sesuatu hal atau mengetahui cara melakukan sesuatu

Contoh: Birds can fly

I can speak two languages


2. Untuk mengungkapkan kemungkinan

Contoh: It can get cold there at night so take a jacket.


3. Untuk menawarkan sesuatu kepada orang lain

Contoh: Can I carry your bags for you?


4. Untuk menanyakan atau memberikan ijin

Contoh: You can use my umbrella, I don’t need it right now.


May

1. Untuk mengungkapkan kemungkinan di masa depan

Contoh: He’s very good, in fact, I think he may win the competition


2. Untuk memberikan ijin

Contoh: You may take only one brochure


3. Untuk menanyakan ijin

Contoh: May I sit next to you?


4. Untuk membicarakan tentang hal-hal yang sering terjadi

Contoh: Drivers may feel tired if they do not take a break every 2 hours


5. Spekulasi tentang masa lampau (may + have + past participle)

Contoh: She is late. I think she may have missed her plane.


6. Untuk mengungkapkan keinginan

Contoh: May you both live a long and happy life together.


Pembahasan

Lucy: Hey Alyssa can you help me to organize the party for my birthday next week?

Alyssa: Sure Lucy! I’d love to do that.

Lucy: So, the party should be held in my house by the pool because this is how I would like it to be.

Alyssa: Sounds awesome. But we must be sure that it won’t be raining, or it will just ruin our effort.

Lucy: You’re right, Alyssa. Okay I will check the forecast later so we can plan the other idea in case of raining.

Alyssa: Oh by the way, may I invite my sister to your party?

Lucy: Sure! It would be fun to have more people. Oh before I forgot, all the guests have to wear a dress code. And it will be anything white.

Alyssa: No problem!

34. mohon bantuannya make a conversations by using gerund ,infitives and modal verbs ?​


Jawaban:

Conversation Questions Gerunds and Infinitives  

Penjelasan:

We use gerunds (verb + ing):

After certain verbs – I enjoy drawing

After prepositions – I drank a beer after running.

As the subject or object of a sentence – Jogging is good exercise

We use ‘to’ + infinitive:

After certain verbs – We decided to buy the house.

After many adjectives – It’s easy to do it.

– I came to London to work in a restaurant.

We use the bare infinitive (the infinitive without ‘to’):

After modal verbs – I can see you in the afternoon.

After ‘let’, ‘make’ and (sometimes) ‘help’ – The teacher let us go home after the test.

After some verbs of perception (see, watch, hear, notice, feel, sense) – I watched her feed the birds.

After expressions with ‘why’ – why waste time in the bar?

1. What food have you never eaten but would really like to try?
