5 contoh possessive pronoun its !

1. The cat has long tail. It's tail is long. The long tail is its.

2. The book has blue color. The cover is thick. The thick cover is its.

3. The computer has hard casing. The hard casing is good. The good hard casing is its.

4. The table has brown color. The surface  is light. The brown light surface is its.

5. The wall is painted green. The green color is wonderful. The wonderful green color is its.

Penjelasan:

Possessive pronoun is kata ganti kepemilikan, yaitu mine, yours, his, hers, ours, theirs, its.

Sedang adjective pronoun is kata keterangan kepemilikan yaitu, my, our, his, her, our, their, it's.
