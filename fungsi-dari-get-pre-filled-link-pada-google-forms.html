22. complete the sentence by using passive forms of the verbs In parentheses The Forbidden City_____ (fill) with beautiful paintings and artifactsA. is fillB.are fillC.is filledD.are filled​


Jawaban :

Arti :

Pesan :

Semoga membantu

Answer :

Are filled

Explanation :

1. Penggunaan simple present karena sifatnya fakta.

Active => S+verb 1(s/es)+ O

Passive => S+tobe(is/am/are)+Verb 3 + by Agent
