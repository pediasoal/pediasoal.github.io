29. tolong bantuiin limit diketakhinggaan fungsi trigonometrilim x-> inf x^2(sec 2/x - 1)ty


Nilai dari [tex]\displaystyle{ \lim_{x \to \infty} x^2\left ( sec\frac{2}{x}-1 \right ) }[/tex] adalah 2.

PEMBAHASAN

Teorema pada limit adalah sebagai berikut :

[tex](i)~\lim\limits_{x \to c} f(x)=f(c)[/tex]

[tex](ii)~\lim\limits_{x \to c} kf(x)=k\lim\limits_{x \to c} f(x)[/tex]

[tex](iii)~\lim\limits_{x \to c} [f(x)\pm g(x)]=\lim\limits_{x \to c} f(x)\pm\lim\limits_{x \to c} g(x)[/tex]

[tex](iv)~\lim\limits_{x \to c} [f(x)\times g(x)]=\lim\limits_{x \to c} f(x)\times\lim\limits_{x \to c} g(x)[/tex]

[tex](v)~\lim\limits_{x \to c} \left [ \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} \right ]=\frac{\lim\limits_{x \to c} f(x)}{\lim\limits_{x \to c} g(x)}[/tex]

[tex](vi)~\lim\limits_{x \to c} \left [ f(x) \right ]^n=\left [ \lim\limits_{x \to c} f(x) \right ]^n[/tex]

Rumus untuk limit fungsi trigonometri :

[tex](i)~\lim\limits_{x \to 0} \frac{sinax}{bx}=\lim\limits_{x \to 0} \frac{tanax}{bx}=\frac{a}{b}[/tex]

[tex](ii)~\lim\limits_{x \to 0} \frac{ax}{sinbx}=\lim\limits_{x \to 0} \frac{ax}{tanbx}=\frac{a}{b}[/tex]

[tex](iii)~\lim\limits_{x \to 0} \frac{sinax}{sinbx}=\lim\limits_{x \to 0} \frac{tanax}{tanbx}=\frac{a}{b}[/tex]

[tex](iv)~\lim\limits_{x \to a} \frac{sin(x-a)}{(x-a)}=\lim\limits_{x \to a} \frac{tan(x-a)}{(x-a)}=1[/tex]

.

DIKETAHUI

[tex]\displaystyle{ \lim_{x \to \infty} x^2\left ( sec\frac{2}{x}-1 \right )= }[/tex]

.

DITANYA

Tentukan nilai limitnya.

.

PENYELESAIAN

Misal y = [tex]\frac{1}{x}[/tex]. Karena x → [tex]\infty[/tex] maka y → [tex]\frac{1}{\infty}=0[/tex].

[tex]\displaystyle{ \lim_{x \to \infty} x^2\left ( sec\frac{2}{x}-1 \right ) }[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{= \lim_{y \to 0} \frac{1}{y^2}\left ( sec2y-1 \right ) }[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{= \lim_{y \to 0} \frac{1}{y^2}\left ( \frac{1}{cos2y}-1 \right ) }[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{= \lim_{y \to 0} \frac{1}{y^2}\left ( \frac{1-cos2y}{cos2y} \right ) }[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{= \lim_{y \to 0} \frac{1}{y^2}\left ( \frac{1-(1-2sin^2y)}{cos2y} \right ) }[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{= \lim_{y \to 0} \frac{1}{y^2}\left ( \frac{2sin^2y}{cos2y} \right ) }[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{= 2\lim_{y \to 0} \frac{sin^2y}{y^2}\times\lim_{y \to 0} \frac{1}{cos2y} }[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{=2\lim_{y \to 0} \left ( \frac{siny}{y} \right )^2\times\frac{1}{cos2(0)} }[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{=2 \left ( \lim_{y \to 0} \frac{siny}{y} \right )^2\times1 }[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{=2 \left ( 1 \right )^2 }[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{=2 }[/tex]

.

KESIMPULAN

Nilai dari [tex]\displaystyle{ \lim_{x \to \infty} x^2\left ( sec\frac{2}{x}-1 \right ) }[/tex] adalah 2.

.
