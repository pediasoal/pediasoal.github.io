Pembahasan Present Future Tense (Masa akan datang)The present future tense is using to express action in the future tenseatau untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian yang akan terjadi pada masa akan datang. Pattern:

(+) S + Will/shall + V1 + Obj + Complement(pelengkap)

(-) S + Will/shall + not +V1 +obj + Compl.

(?) Will/shall + S + V1 +obj + Compl ?

Yes, …. No, ….Catatan:Will not = won’t Shallnot = shan’t

——

Will = I, you, we, they, he, she, it. Shall = I and you

Pembahasan Future Be Going To Digunakan untuk menyatakan sebuah rencana yang akan dilakukan di masa depanPolanya yaitu sama (Will = be going to)Pattern:

(+) S + Be (is/am/are) + Going To + V1 + O + Complement

(-) S + Be (is/am/are) + not + going to + V1 + O/C

(?) Be (is/am/are) + S + going to + V1 +obj + C?

Catatan Be

=> Be am, untuk Subjek “I”

=> Be is, untuk Subjek “He/She/it/satu mama”

=> Be are, untuk Subjek “You/we/they/dua nama”

Common Time (ket. Waktu) FUTURE Tonight, soon, in a few minutes, tomorrow, next (week/month,year))Contohnya:

1. I will see you tomorrow.

Atau: I am going to see you tomorrow

2. We will/shall come to your house next week.

3. People will be safer when they drive in slower cars.

(+) I will see you tonight.

(-) I won’t see you tonight.

(?) Will you see me tonight ?

=> yes, I will

=> No, I won’t
