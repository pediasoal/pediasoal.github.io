1. Define of the following terms 1. Capital 2. Cost benefit analysis 3. Factor of production 4. Human capital 5. Market


Jawaban:

1. wealth in the form of money or other assets owned by a person or organization or available or contributed for a particular purpose such as starting a company or investing.

2. A cost-benefit analysis is a process businesses use to analyze decisions. The business or analyst sums the benefits of a situation or action and then subtracts the costs associated with taking that action.

3. In economics, factors of production, resources, or inputs are what is used in the production process to produce output—that is, finished goods and services.

4. Human capital is the stock of habits, knowledge, social and personality attributes (including creativity) embodied in the ability to perform labour so as to produce economic value.

5. A market is one of the many varieties of systems, institutions, procedures, social relations and infrastructures whereby parties engage in exchange.

29. In 1905 Juneau replaced Sitka O a. the capital was O b. was the capital of O c. the capital being O d. as the capital of Alaska​


Jawaban:

B. was the capital of

Penjelasan:

The correct option is b. "was the capital of." The sentence states that in 1905, Juneau replaced Sitka as the capital of Alaska. The verb "replaced" indicates a past action, and "was the capital of" accurately describes Juneau's role as the capital during that time.
